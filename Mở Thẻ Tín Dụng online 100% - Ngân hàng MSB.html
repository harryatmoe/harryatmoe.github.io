Incident description

In 2019, the msb.com.vn website experienced a data breach. Over 1 million phone numbers were exposed, together with personal and location data. Leaked records include ID number, address, date of birth, name, gender and email address. The validity of the data exposed couldn’t be verified. Yet we’re still informing you about a potential data breach – but keep in mind there’s a chance of it being a false positive.
